Business Problem

Organizations need a clear way to track client activity, contract status, and revenue performance. Without a dashboard, leadership may struggle to see where opportunities are growing and where bottlenecks exist.

Tools Used

Power BI, Excel

Dashboard Features

  • KPI cards for total clients, active contracts, and revenue
  • Bar chart for client status distribution
  • Line chart for contract trends over time
  • Revenue breakdown by industry
  • Detailed client table for operational tracking

Key Insights

The dashboard revealed where client activity was concentrated, which industries generated the most value, and where pipeline slowdowns could limit business growth.

Business Impact

This project improved reporting visibility and supported better decision-making by helping leadership focus on high-value opportunities and monitor pipeline health more effectively.
