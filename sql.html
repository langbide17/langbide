Business Problem

Businesses need to know which products and regions perform best so they can improve strategy, increase revenue, and allocate resources more effectively.

Tools Used

SQL, MySQL

Analysis Performed

  • Grouped revenue by region
  • Ranked top-performing products
  • Analyzed monthly sales trends
  • Used aggregation functions to summarize performance

Key Insights

The analysis highlighted which regions generated the most revenue, which products had the strongest performance, and how sales changed over time.

Business Impact

These findings can help a business focus on top-performing products, strengthen regional strategy, and prepare for seasonal patterns in demand.
